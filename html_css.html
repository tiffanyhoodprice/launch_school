Pear and Bacon Grilled Cheese

Ingredients:

2 tablespoons unsalted butter

2 tablespoons currant or fig jam

2 spces white sandwich bread

2 spces Cheddar

2 spces cooked bacon

1/4 small pear, thinly spced

Directions:

1. Melt the butter in a small skillet over medium-low heat. Spread the jam on 1 spce of the bread and form a sandwich with the cheese, bacon, and pear.

2. Cook the sandwich, covered, until the bread is toasted and the cheese is melted, 2 to 3 minutes per side.
